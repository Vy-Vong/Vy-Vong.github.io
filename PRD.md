## 1. Overview
<!-- 
This is a brief description of the website and its overall purpose
-->
**Website Name:** Vy Vong   
**Purpose:** To showcase Vy Vong’s artwork, creative projects, and exhibitions in a clean, visually engaging online portfolio. The site will also provide contact information for works, collaborations, and professional inquiries.
**Target Audience:** Illusatrions, galleries, 3D animators, and potential clients seeking original artwork or collaborations.

## 2. Goals
<!-- 
Clearly state objectives/goals. These should be measurable or at least clearly defined.
-->
- Present  artwork in a visually compelling and organized format.

- Allow visitors to easily view artwork by category or medium.

- Provide clear access to contact and social media information.

- Highlight artist background, inspiration, and creative process.

- Optimize the website for both mobile and desktop users.

## 3. Features
<!-- 
List the key features the website will have. Each feature should include a short description. 
-->
1. **Home Section**  
   - Brief call-to-action button linking to “Projects” or “Résumé.”
   - Hero image or clean intro banner with name, title (e.g., “Designer & Creative Technologist”), and tagline.  

2. **Skills Section**  
   - Artwork displayed in grid or masonry layout.
   - Filter or category options (e.g., Painting, Illustration, Mixed Media, Digital).
   - Lightbox functionality for larger image viewing.
   - List or icon grid of technical and creative skills (e.g., HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Adobe Suite, UX Design).
   - Option to include proficiency bars or categories.

3. **About Section**  
   - Artist biography and artist statement.
   - Education, exhibitions, and awards (if applicable).
   - Optional artist choices.
   - Profile photo and short statement about work philosophy or creative approach.

4. **Project Section**  
   - Highlight ongoing or past art projects, collaborations, or installations.
   - Grid or card layout showcasing selected projects.
   - Optional hover effect or lightbox for visuals.

5. **Contract Section**  
   - Contact form for commissions or questions.
   - Phone number and email.  
   - Social media links - including Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube.  
   - Email and social media links (Instagram, TikTok, Behance, etc.).

6. **Footer**  
   - Quicknavigation links (Home, Gallery, About, Contact).
   - Social icons
   - Copyrights notice.

## 5. Design 
<!-- 
Include basic design and user experience considerations. You can include color schemes, fonts, layouts, etc.
-->
- **Brand aesthetic** Clean, modern, Cerative, and confident — balanced between creative and professional.
Color Scheme: Neutral base (white/gray/black) with one accent color (e.g., #A60512 or #1901ecff) for emphasis.
- **Color Scheme:** Modernized Italian color scheme with accent: #c90e0eff, #0051ffff, #F2B705
- **Typography:** Lateef (updated Roman typeface) for headings, Alata (sans-serif) for body text, Headings: Poppins or Playfair Display for elegance
Body: Inter or Alata for clarity and readability
- **Navigation:** Fixed top bar with links to Home, About, Projects, Skills, Résumé, Contact, hamburger menu for mobile devices
- **Layout:** SSimple scrollable layout with consistent spacing, hero banner at top followed by clearly defined sections  
- **Images:** High-quality, High-resolution thumbnails of projects.
